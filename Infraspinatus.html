Infraspinatus

Infraspinatus is a muscle that is found at the shoulder region of the upper limb.It originates at the infraspinous fossa of the scapular.The infraspinatus inserts at the cupsule of the shoulder joint and the greater tuberosity of the humerus.Its nerve supply comes from the suprascapular nerve
